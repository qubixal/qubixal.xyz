01 Story / 故事

Who is this cube?

qubixal started as a Youtube channel aiming to explore hardware, software and creative technology. From custom PCBs to local LLM deployments, from useless inventions to impressive ones - this is the qubixal manifesto.

q比(qubixal)是一位未来up主，旨在探索硬件、软件和创意技术的交汇点。 从定制PCB到本地LLM部署，从看似无用的发明到令人惊艳的杰作——这就是qubixal思想。
